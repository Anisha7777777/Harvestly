About Harvestly

Harvestly bridges the gap between farmers and consumers by providing a direct, transparent, and efficient platform for buying and selling fresh produce. Our goal is to ensure farmers get fair prices while consumers enjoy fresh, high-quality produce.

Farmers can register, list their products, and showcase their farm locations on our interactive map. Consumers can browse products, compare prices, and place orders directly with farmers, selecting convenient delivery slots.
